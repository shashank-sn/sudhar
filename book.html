What it’s about

You can’t document what doesn’t exist.

Most personal branding advice teaches you how to talk about the work. This book is about doing the work that’s worth talking about — the inner-work, the identity shifts, the daily practice that makes a person worthy of being a brand.

Written from 14 years of building businesses, training 35,000+ entrepreneurs across 12 countries, and 200+ keynotes including TEDx.

Inside the book

The frameworks. The reps. The why.

Each chapter is built around a framework that has worked in the field — in cohorts, intensives, and 1:1 engagements with founders, CXOs, coaches, and operators.
