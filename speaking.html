The backstory
25 years of stuttering · 1 identity shift · massive lesson

For twenty-five years I couldn’t put two words together.
Today I teach people how to find theirs.

I stuttered until I was twenty-five. I hadn’t been on a stage until I turned 29. The irony isn’t lost on me that speaking ended up being the thing I’d build a life around.

Somewhere along the way I stopped trying to fix the stutter and started getting aligned with who I really was. Once I worked on my identity, everything started falling into place. And the stuttering disappeared.

I bring that experience to every talk: not just the frameworks for personal branding and positioning, but the felt truth of what it takes to show up with something to say.
