The backstory
25 years · 200 stages · 1 lesson

For twenty-five years I was terrified of my own voice.
Today I teach people how to find theirs.

I stuttered until I was twenty-five. Every word was a gamble. Every stage was a threat. The irony isn’t lost on me that speaking ended up being the thing I’d build a life around.

Somewhere along the way I stopped trying to fix the stutter and started addressing what was underneath — anxiety. Once I gave the anxiety a seat at the table, the words found their rhythm.

I bring that experience to every talk: not just the frameworks for personal branding and positioning, but the felt truth of what it takes to show up with something to say.
